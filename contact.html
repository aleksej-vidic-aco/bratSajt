Lokacija

Whether you want to purchase a piece you've seen on my website, or are interested in commissioning an original work, the best way to start is to simply call or e-mail me so we can discuss exactly what it is you'd like.

Most of the works you see on my site and in my studio are available. Some, like the marble pieces, are one-of-a-kind, while others in bronze and terracotta are limited editions. contact me and I'll be happy to answer your questions and get you what you need. Works are available in a variety of finishes and media.

Enlargements, reductions, and edition pieces are also available for many works.
